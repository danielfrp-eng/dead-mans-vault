How it works

Simple setup. Automatic protection.

Three steps to make sure your most important information reaches the right person — even if you can't.

Create your vault
Set a PIN and fingerprint lock. Add your secrets — passwords, documents, personal messages, access codes. Everything is encrypted immediately with AES-256-GCM.
Set your check-in
Choose how often you want to check in — from 1 hour to 30 days. Add an emergency contact who will receive your secrets if you miss your deadline.
Automatic delivery
If you miss a check-in, your vault sends your secrets to your emergency contact via Gmail. No servers involved — it happens entirely from your device.
Stay in control
As long as you check in regularly, your secrets stay private. Change your PIN, add contacts, update secrets, or adjust your interval any time from Settings.

FAQ

Common questions

What happens if I forget to check in? ▼
The app sends an email with your decrypted secrets to your emergency contact(s) via your own Gmail account. You receive push notification reminders before the deadline — when 50% of your interval has passed, and again at 80%. The email is only sent if you miss the final deadline completely.
Can Dead Man's Vault see my secrets? ▼
No. Your secrets are encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a key that is stored in the Android Hardware Security Module — a physical chip on your device. The key never leaves your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your data.
What if I lose my phone? ▼
Pro subscribers can restore their vault on a new device using the encrypted backup from Google Drive. The backup is protected by your PIN — even if someone gains access to your Drive, they cannot read the backup without it. Free users can still recover their PIN using recovery codes if needed.
What if I forget my PIN? ▼
You have three recovery options: (1) use your fingerprint to confirm your identity and set a new PIN, (2) use one of the 8 recovery codes generated when you set up your vault, or (3) if you're a Pro subscriber, re-authenticate with Google and restore from your Drive backup.
Does the app require an internet connection? ▼
No. The core vault functionality — storing, viewing, and managing secrets, checking in, and PIN authentication — works entirely offline. Internet is only required for the Gmail send when the dead man's switch triggers, and for Google Drive backup/restore on Pro.
How is this different from a password manager? ▼
Password managers are designed to help you access your passwords conveniently. Dead Man's Vault is designed to ensure someone else can access your information if something happens to you. It's not a replacement for a password manager — it's a complement to it, focused on emergency access and digital estate planning.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription? ▼
Yes, at any time through your Google Play account. Your Pro features remain active until the end of the billing period. After cancellation, your vault reverts to free tier limits — your existing secrets are not deleted, but you won't be able to add new ones beyond the free limit.
