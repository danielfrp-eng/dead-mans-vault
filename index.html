Dead Man's Vault

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Dead Man's Vault does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All your secrets and settings are encrypted locally on your device and never leave it without your explicit action.

1. Who we are

Dead Man's Vault ("the App", "we", "our") is an Android application developed and published as an individual developer. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at the address listed in section 8.

2. What data we do NOT collect

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any of the following:

The content of your secrets or vault entries

Your PIN or biometric data

Your name, email address, or emergency contact details

Location data, device identifiers, or usage analytics

Crash logs or diagnostic data sent to our servers

Advertising identifiers beyond what Google AdMob manages autonomously (free tier only)

3. Where your data is stored

All data entered into the App is stored exclusively on your device, encrypted with AES-256-GCM using the Android Hardware Security Module. The encryption key never leaves your device.

Optional Google Drive backup (Pro tier)

If you choose to enable the backup feature, an encrypted copy of your vault is written to your personal Google Drive account, in the private appDataFolder space only accessible by this App. We have no access to this folder.

4. Emergency email delivery

If the dead man's switch is triggered, the App composes an email on your behalf using your own Google account via the Gmail API. This action is performed entirely on your device. We never see, intercept, or store the content of these emails.

6. Device transfer (Pro tier)

Pro subscribers can transfer their vault to a new device by restoring the encrypted backup from Google Drive. No data passes through our servers during this process.

7. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your rights (GDPR)

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, most GDPR rights are exercised directly on your device by deleting the App or using "Delete vault" in Settings.

Email: privacy@deadmansvault.app

We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR Article 12.

9. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be notified via an in-app notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
